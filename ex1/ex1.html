Exercise One

In Wild Wallpaper, I included nested "for loop" and "if" statements and combined different aspects of the class examples.

This exercise took me a long time because I kept confusing different parts of the code. I also struggled a lot with the colors. The project did not fully turn out how I wanted it to. I wanted the dots and lines to be separate and I wanted to make the dots colored. I also wish I changed the spacing and some of the colors of the shapes.

I really like the color and translucency of the squares on the bottom left and I like the color scheme of the ellipses on the left.
